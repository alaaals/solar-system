Instructions:

To play this simulation, please select a star from what is displayed in order to enter your very own solar system.


Once you're inside your solar system, start customizing the color and size scale to fit what you want, then drag and place whichever object you'd like into the solar system for it to start orbiting. To add moons to planets, simply click on the moon button, then click on the planet in which you'd like to add the moon to. You can place any number of moons to one planet!
